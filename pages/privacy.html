Home Privacy Policy

Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

When you use the platform, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and account details. In addition, any media you upload — such as audio, images, or video — is processed by our system to generate creatures.

We also collect general usage data, such as how you interact with features, to improve the overall experience.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is primarily used to power the core experience of the platform. This includes generating creatures from your inputs, improving system performance, and personalizing your experience.

We may also use your data to communicate with you, respond to inquiries, and ensure the platform remains secure and reliable.

AI Processing

To create creatures, your uploaded content is analyzed by our AI systems. This process is automated and designed specifically to transform your input into creative outputs.

We do not use your content for unrelated purposes, and any processing is limited to improving the functionality of the platform.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information through secure systems and controlled access. While no system can guarantee complete security, we continuously work to keep your data safe.

Data Retention

We keep your data only for as long as it is necessary to provide our services and meet legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, it is securely removed.

Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect improvements or changes in the platform. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Welcome to the Hoheto Kai FAQ section! Here you'll find answers to common questions about how to use the platform, manage your account, share content, and connect with the community.

It's an AI platform where you can generate creatures from sound, audio, images, and videos.

Upload a sound, image, or video and our AI engine processes your input to generate a unique creature card tailored to the characteristics of your media.

Hoheto Kai runs entirely in your browser. A modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) with a stable internet connection is all you need.

Yes! The platform is fully responsive and works on iOS and Android browsers. A dedicated mobile app is coming soon.

We support MP3, WAV, OGG for audio; JPG, PNG, WEBP for images; and MP4, MOV, WEBM for video. Maximum file size is 50MB per upload.
